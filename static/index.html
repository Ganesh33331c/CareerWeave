// agent architecture

Four agents.
One seamless pipeline.

Each agent has a single responsibility. Together they turn a raw resume and a job search query into a fully optimised career package.

AGENT_01
🔍
Scout
Queries the SerpApi Google Jobs engine to find the top live job matching your search term. Returns title, company, location, and full job description.
↳ SerpApi · Google Jobs Engine
AGENT_02
📊
Analyst
Cross-references your resume keywords against the JD vocabulary. Produces an ATS match score 0–100 with a 2-sentence reasoning explanation.
↳ Gemini 1.5 Flash · LangChain
AGENT_03
✍️
Architect
Rewrites your resume's professional summary and 5 key bullets using JD-matching language, action verbs, and quantified impact statements.
↳ Gemini 1.5 Flash · PromptTemplate
AGENT_04
🎯
Coach
Generates 3 highly specific technical and behavioural interview questions grounded in the exact technologies and responsibilities of the target JD.
↳ Gemini 1.5 Flash · Structured JSON
